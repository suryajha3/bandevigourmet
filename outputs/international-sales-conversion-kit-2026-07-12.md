# International Sales Conversion Kit

Prepared: July 12, 2026

This kit turns a website or directory lead into a structured buyer conversation. Use it only with final, owner-approved business information.

## Lead Stages

1. New lead: Buyer submits the International Buyer Desk, Buyer Catalog, wholesale, or Canada enquiry.
2. Qualified: Buyer confirms business type, market, product interest, expected volume, and timing.
3. Sample review: Buyer confirms sample need, destination city, courier preference, and product mix.
4. Commercial review: Product format, MOQ, price, freight basis, labels, documents, payment terms, and production time are reviewed in writing.
5. Quote sent: A written quote is sent from the official BandEvi business email.
6. Follow-up: Follow up after 4 working days, then close or move forward based on the buyer response.

## Daily Sales Routine

1. Check new website enquiries twice each business day.
2. Reply to qualified leads within one business day using the official business email.
3. Record buyer name, country, product interest, stage, date contacted, and next follow-up date.
4. Send only the Buyer Catalog and Canada / market page until official documents are ready.
5. Keep price, MOQ, certificates, registration numbers, and freight promises out of public or first-contact messages until approved.

## Final Inputs Required Before Quotes

- Official BandEvi business email
- Official phone / WhatsApp number
- Legal entity name and registered address
- Final pack sizes and product photographs
- GST, FSSAI, and IEC details when officially issued
- Owner-approved product pricing, MOQ, payment terms, and sample policy
