## Follow-Up Rhythm

1. Send the initial message with the Canada page and Buyer Catalog.
2. After 4 working days, send a short follow-up asking whether the buyer handles Indian snack or specialty grocery additions.
3. After a positive reply, ask for buyer type, destination city, sales channel, sample need, target pack format, and expected volume.
4. Only then discuss price, MOQ, documents, freight, and production timing by written quote.
