Our story

Built to make Indian pantry products feel clear, premium, and dependable.

BandEvi Gourmet is being shaped as a modern Indian food brand for families, retail shelves, gifting, and wholesale buyers who want familiar pantry staples with better presentation and clearer trust signals.

The focus is intentionally simple: roasted makhana, poha, daily masala, non-veg masala, herbal spice notes, whole spice staples, and curated bundles. These are products customers understand, but the brand experience around them should feel organized, trackable, and ready for a wider market.

We are building the website as a working commerce system: customers can browse separate product pages, add items to cart, create a booking ID, track status, raise support requests, and reorder from their account. For business buyers, wholesale enquiry and support flows are prepared for sampling, carton planning, and export conversations.

Brand promise

The long-term goal is not only to sell packets online. The goal is to make every product feel trustworthy before a customer adds it to cart and every order feel traceable after checkout.
