Buyer process

Simple path from enquiry to quote.

  1. Buyer shares market, product interest, quantity, and sample requirement.
  2. BandEvi reviews pack size, MOQ, destination, and private-label needs.
  3. Written quote, packing details, payment terms, and shipment timeline are confirmed.
  4. Documents are shared only after owner-approved verification.

Quality proof
