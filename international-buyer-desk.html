International buyer types

Each buyer needs a different first answer.

International sales improve when the first reply matches the buyer type. A distributor needs carton and MOQ planning. A retail buyer needs product range and shelf plan. A private-label buyer needs artwork, label, barcode, compliance, and lead-time review.

Distributor / importer

Ask for destination country, expected monthly volume, first sample need, carton preference, product mix, and required documents.

Retail / supermarket buyer

Share product range, pack photos, retail pouch options, ingredient notes, shelf-life fields, and final label verification policy.

Private-label buyer

Confirm artwork, barcode, net weight, ingredients, allergen statement, market rules, MOQ, and approval timeline before quote.

Hotel, caterer, and gifting buyer

Ask for use case, event date, pack size, gift box requirement, volume, destination city, and delivery timeline.
