Bulk makhana FAQ

Practical answers before a bulk quote.

Bulk buyers can use this page to prepare the core details before contacting BandEvi Gourmet Export: pack type, sample need, carton quantity, destination market, label requirements, and proof documents.

Can sample cartons be requested?

Yes. Sample-carton discussion can start after the buyer shares product interest, destination, quantity range, and pack-size preference.

How is MOQ decided?

MOQ depends on pack format, flavor mix, carton plan, private-label needs, lead time, and freight assumptions.

Can buyers discuss private label?

Yes, but private-label work should include artwork, barcode, ingredient, label, MOQ, and compliance review before commitment.

Where should trust verification start?

Start with the Proof Center, company profile, office network, and safe claim policy before asking for sensitive commercial documents.
