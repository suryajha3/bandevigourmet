Publishing discipline

Daily brand updates should be useful, verified, and not spammy.

For SEO, BandEvi Gourmet should publish product education, recipes, packaging updates, buyer FAQs, and verified company milestones. Repeating the same "about us" or net worth message multiple times daily can look low-quality.

Good daily updates

Product guides, recipes, use cases, new pack photos, wholesale FAQs, customer support updates, and verified trust documents.

Financial claims

Net worth, revenue, valuation, or profit claims should only be published after owner approval and documentary verification.

Multiple posts per day

Use social platforms for frequent short updates. Keep website pages high-quality and update them when there is something meaningful to add.
