Cancellation policy

How order cancellation works

Before dispatch

Retail orders may usually be cancelled before dispatch or packing starts. If the order is already packed, dispatched, customized, or handed to the carrier, cancellation may not be available.

After dispatch

Food products are handled carefully for hygiene and safety. After dispatch, cancellation is treated under the refund and return policy instead of automatic cancellation.

Bulk and export orders

Wholesale, private-label, gifting, and export orders may require advance purchase of raw material, packaging, labelling, or freight booking. Cancellation terms should be confirmed in the quote.

Seller cancellation

BandEvi Gourmet may cancel an order if stock is unavailable, payment fails, address is not serviceable, documentation is incomplete, product cannot be legally shipped, or fraud is suspected.

Refund and return policy

Fair handling for damaged, wrong, or missing items

Refund, replacement, or store credit may be considered for damaged packages, wrong products, missing items, duplicate payment, or confirmed quality issues.

Opened, used, tampered, expired after delivery, improperly stored, or customer-damaged food products may not be eligible for return or refund unless required by law.

Approved refunds are usually processed to the original payment method or agreed account within 7 to 14 business days, depending on bank and payment provider timelines.

Mandatory rights

Nothing in this policy removes consumer rights that apply by law in the buyer's country, including rights for defective, misdescribed, or unsafe goods.
