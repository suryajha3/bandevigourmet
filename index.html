About us

A modern Indian food brand for everyday kitchens and global shelves.

Makhana & Masala brings two trusted Indian pantry categories together: roasted fox nuts for clean snacking and spice blends for daily cooking. The brand is built for families, gifting, retail counters, hospitality buyers, and distributors who want consistent taste with premium presentation.

Our direction is simple: source carefully, process in small batches, pack neatly, and make Indian flavors easier to understand for buyers across markets.
